A botnet is a network of compromised devices controlled remotely by attackers through command-and-control (C2) servers.

Key Characteristics:
- Centralized or decentralized control
- Large-scale coordination
- Remote command execution

Common Behaviors:
- DDoS attacks
- Spam campaigns
- Credential harvesting
- Cryptomining
- Data exfiltration

Indicators:
- Unusual outbound traffic
- Periodic C2 connections
- High network usage
- Unknown background processes
